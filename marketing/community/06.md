Title: Built a free Korean market analysis tool — looking for feedback

Hey r/korea,

I built a free tool for anyone interested in Korean online markets and wanted to share it here for feedback.

wealthpipe.net covers a few different areas:

- Naver keyword analyzer: search volume, competition score, opportunity score, AI-suggested article structure, and 12-month trend. Useful if you run a Korean blog or Naver Smart Store.
- KRX stock screener: filter through 2,700+ Korean stocks by factor metrics (PER, PBR, ROE, etc.)
- Asset signal tracker: KOSPI, S&P500, and Bitcoin at a glance
- Daily economic news digest: AI-summarized Korean financial news

Everything is free, no login required.

I built this because I was frustrated with how scattered Korean market data tools are. Happy to hear what's missing or what could be improved.

Would appreciate any feedback from people who deal with Korean markets, whether for blogging, investing, or e-commerce.

Site: wealthpipe.net
